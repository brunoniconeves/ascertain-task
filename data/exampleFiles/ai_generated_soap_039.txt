SOAP Note - Synthetic Example #039
Title: Skin rash

S:
Itchy rash on forearms x1 week after new detergent. No fever. No mucosal involvement.

O:
Skin: erythematous, mildly scaly patches on bilateral forearms, no vesicles.

A:
Contact dermatitis.

P:
Stop suspected irritant; topical steroid BID x7-10 days.
Antihistamine PRN itching. Return if spreading or signs of infection.
